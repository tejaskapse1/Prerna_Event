Welcome to

Dr. S. C. Gulhane Prerna College of Commerce, Science & Arts

Prerna Sewa Mandal, one of the pioneer organisations dedicated to the cause of education from its very inception, started its operations in the year 1974. This society has successfully completed 4 decades in the field of teaching and is recognised and appreciated for its steady development, achievements in the region. The formal body of the society which came into existence in 1983, aims for an all-round growth of the institution, as all are reputed academicians.

The Secretary of Prerna Sewa Mandal Dr. S. C. Gulhane who has always been a constant source of inspiration, tirelessly endeavours for the development of the college. He believes in not only educating the students but in making them self-reliant members of the society. His future vision is to bring up the Prerna Junior College and Prerna Senior College as one of the highly reputed Colleges in East and South Nagpur. The horizon may seem far but the sun does manage to reach there.
